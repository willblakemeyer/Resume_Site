Sumo

Description: This was about the first notable project I've made, where I created a two-player physics game. The goal of the game is simple: knock your opponent out of the ring to win (just like in Sumo wrestling). To make the game more interesting, I gave both players unique abilities, where the larger player is able to boost himself by holding the spacebar, and the smaller player is able to make a clone of himself by pressing the forward slash key.
